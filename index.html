ZZ Plant Care Guide

Soil for ZZ Plant

Well-draining, all-purpose potting mix.

ZZ Plants thrive in well-draining soil; a standard all-purpose potting mix is suitable for their growth.

Watering

Allow the soil to dry out between waterings.

ZZ Plants are drought-tolerant; water sparingly and let the soil dry before watering again.

Lighting

Low to bright, indirect light.

ZZ Plants can adapt to various light conditions, from low to bright indirect light, but avoid direct sunlight.

Benefits

Tolerant of low light and neglect.

ZZ Plants are known for their resilience and ability to thrive in low-light environments with minimal care.

Effective air purifier.

ZZ Plants help improve indoor air quality by removing pollutants from the air.

Non-Benefits

Toxic to pets if ingested; keep out of reach of animals.
